Who is gabeyJ?

Gabe Avrom Jacobs, (born August 15, 2001) known in-game as simply "wombat", is a Jewish professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player currently playing the AWPer role for inDirect.gg. He was named "eSports Player of the Year" at The Game Awards 2015. He was a pro League of Legands player before transitioning to CS:GO in 2012. gabeyJ is considered one of the best AWPers in the world. The other teams he played for include Odyssey, Mideval, and IND. He is best known for his incredible carry performances online and at lan during his era in Odyssey and early days on team inDirect.gg, despite changes in the game making his playstyle more difficult.
